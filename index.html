About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student at NYU Abu Dhabi interested in applied artificial intelligence and emerging technologies. Over time, I realized that what excites me most is not just learning new tools, but building systems that actually work outside of a classroom or research setting.

My work sits between research and deployment. I enjoy taking complex ideas and turning them into usable systems, whether that means building a retrieval based chatbot, designing backend APIs, or experimenting with autonomous agents. I care about reliability, clarity, and building things that people can actually use.

I am especially interested in technology that improves access, whether in mental health, automation, or digital infrastructure. For me, engineering is about making powerful systems practical and accessible.
