About Me

Engineer • Researcher • Builder

I'm Calvin Gutsa, a Computer Engineering student at NYU Abu Dhabi with a deep interest in emerging technologies, mental health innovation, and socially impactful solutions. My portfolio spans embedded systems, research-driven software, and intuitive backend design.

Originally from Zimbabwe and currently based in the UAE, I’ve worked across labs, startups, and research teams to bring ideas to life through thoughtful, scalable tech. I’m passionate about solving meaningful problems through systems thinking and cross-disciplinary design.

Whether it’s prototyping a USSD platform for low-connectivity regions or developing emotion-aware chatbots, I care about building tools that matter. Let's connect and collaborate!
